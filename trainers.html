TRAIN THE FIGHTER IN YOU

Our gym trainers are certified fitness professionals with the knowledge and experience to help you reach your fitness goals. They stay up-to-date on exercise science and safety protocols.

Cources Available

FITNESS CLASSES

  • Group exercise classes like yoga, pilates, zumba, spin/cycling, bootcamp, etc.
  • Some gyms have pools and offer aqua aerobics or other water-based classes.
  • Fitness classes offer a dynamic and motivating environment for achieving your health goals.
  • From high-energy cardio sessions to mind-body practices like yoga and Pilates, there's a class for every fitness level and interest.
  • With certified instructors leading the way, you'll not only challenge your body but also enjoy the camaraderie and support of fellow participants on your wellness journey.

WEIGHT TRAINING

  • Free weight areas with barbells, dumbbells, benches.
  • Machine weight circuit areas.
  • Some gyms have dedicated areas for powerlifting, Olympic lifting.
  • Weight training is a powerful way to build strength, improve muscle tone, and boost overall fitness levels.
  • By lifting weights, you can increase bone density, enhance metabolism, and even reduce the risk of injury in daily activities.

CARDIO

  • Treadmills
  • Ellipticals
  • Stair climbers
  • Rowing machines
  • Stationary bikes
  • Cardiovascular exercise, or cardio, is essential for improving heart health, burning calories, and enhancing endurance.

PRE-WORKOUT

  • Ingredients: Banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey.
  • Benefits: Provides quick energy from natural sugars, contains protein for muscle repair, and hydration from almond milk.
  • Ingredients: Rolled oats, almond milk, mixed berries, honey.
  • Benefits: Slow-release carbs for sustained energy, antioxidants from berries, and a touch of sweetness from honey.

POST-WORKOUT

  • Ingredients: Grilled chicken breast, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, balsamic vinaigrette.
  • Benefits: Lean protein for muscle repair, veggies for vitamins and minerals, and hydration from the salad.
  • Ingredients: Cooked quinoa, black beans, avocado, bell peppers, cilantro.
  • Benefits: Protein from quinoa and black beans, healthy fats from avocado, and carbs for replenishing glycogen stores.

OTHER-AMENITIES

  • Basketball/racquetball courts
  • Swimming pools
  • Saunas/steam rooms
  • Childcare
  • Smoothie bars or small cafes
  • In addition to fitness equipment and classes, many gyms offer a variety of amenities to enhance the overall experience.
